description: Learn how to add team members to your Sourcingmentor project.
---

## Overview

Invite your coworkers to collaborate on projects in Sourcingmentor.

## Steps

1. Open your project dashboard.
2. Click on the "Team" tab.
3. Select "Invite Members".
4. Enter team member email addresses.
5. Click "Send Invites".

<Callout kind="info">
Invited users will receive an email with a join link.
</Callout>
