---
title: Create Your First Project
description: Quickly start and manage a project in Sourcingmentor.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Go to `https://app.sourcingmentor.com` and sign in with your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Project'">
    On the dashboard, press the "New Project" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set project details">
    Enter a project name, description, and select a template if desired.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click "Create Project" to finish.
  </Step>
</Steps>
